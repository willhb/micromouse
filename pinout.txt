LPC2148 Pinouts for 3pi Micromouse

See: http://openlpc.com/4e26f1/hardware2148

P0.0		TXD0		TX to 3pi
P0.1		RXD0		RX from 3pi

P0.6		AD1.0		Wall Sensor

P0.8		TXD1		TX to XBee
P0.9		RXD1		RX from Xbee

P0.10		AD1.2		Wall Sensor
P0.12		AD1.3		Wall Sensor
P0.13		AD1.4		Wall Sensor

P0.14		Button		OpenLPC User Button

P0.15		EINT0		Wheel Encoder
P0.16		EINT3		Wheel Encoder
